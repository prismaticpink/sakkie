Off-Platform Webring

About

Off-Platform Webring is a webring for adults who own sites with a custom domain, hosted outside the main hosts. The indie web/small web has some free hosts with social features that help sites stay connected, but those who go through a more DIY path had no community. That's what this webring is for!

Last update: 21/12/2025

Rules

  • You must have a domain and a host with no social features. Sorry, Neocities and Nekoweb sites do not fit this criterion, even if the social features are disabled. There are webrings exclusively for Neocities users and Nekoweb users, but no one for sites outside these platforms, and that's why I created this one.
  • You must be at least 18 years old to join.
  • Please specify if your site has mature/NSFW content. Any content is fine, as long as it is not illegal or hateful.
  • Once approved, grab a code and join us!
